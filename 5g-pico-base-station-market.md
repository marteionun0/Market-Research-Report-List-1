<p><p>The 5G Pico Base Station market can be categorized into two main types: Stand Alone (SA) and Non-Stand Alone (NSA). SA networks utilize a dedicated 5G architecture, enabling enhanced features like lower latency and improved capacity without reliance on existing 4G infrastructure. In contrast, NSA networks leverage the existing 4G LTE infrastructure while integrating 5G capabilities, offering quicker deployment and a transitional step toward full 5G. Both types cater to different deployment needs and performance requirements in urban and semi-urban areas.</p></p>
