<p><p>The amorphous silicon solar cells market is primarily divided into two types: single junction and multi-junction. Single junction cells consist of a single layer of amorphous silicon, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, yet with lower efficiency. In contrast, multi-junction cells combine multiple layers of different semiconductor materials to capture a broader spectrum of sunlight, significantly enhancing efficiency. Each type serves distinct applications, with single junctions being popular for small-scale or flexible installations, while multi-junctions are preferred for high-performance systems in specialized environments.</p></p>
