<p><ul><li>Thermal Process</li><li>Dry Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Battery Laminated Film market is categorized into two main types based on the production process: Thermal Process and Dry Process. The Thermal Process involves the application of heat to bond layers of the film, promoting enhanced adhesion and durability suitable for high-performance batteries. In contrast, the Dry Process relies on solvent-free techniques for film lamination, offering a more environmentally friendly option. Both processes aim to optimize the performance and safety of laminated films used in battery applications.</p></p>
