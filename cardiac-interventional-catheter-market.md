<p><p>The cardiac interventional catheter market is primarily divided into two types: vascular and non-vascular interventions. Vascular intervention focuses on procedures targeting blood vessels, such as angioplasty and stent placement, facilitating blood flow in conditions like coronary artery disease. Non-vascular intervention encompasses procedures within the heart, including electrophysiological ablations and valve repairs, addressing issues like arrhythmias and structural heart diseases. Both segments aim to enhance cardiac function and improve patient outcomes through minimally invasive techniques.</p></p>
