<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Entry System (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Keyless Entry Systems market offers two primary types: Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). PKES enables automatic unlocking when the authorized user approaches the vehicle, enhancing convenience and security without requiring manual interaction. In contrast, RKES requires the user to press a button on a remote key fob to unlock or lock the vehicle doors, offering a straightforward and traditional approach. Both systems aim to improve access control and user experience in commercial vehicles.</p></p>
