<p><p>Continuous Non-Invasive Glucose Monitoring Systems (CNGMS) for diabetes are essential tools for managing blood glucose levels without conventional finger-pricking blood tests. The market can be divided into two primary types: wearable CNGMS and implantable CNGMS. Wearable CNGMS includes devices like smartwatches or patches that monitor glucose levels in real-time, providing immediate feedback. In contrast, implantable CNGMS involves devices surgically placed under the skin that continuously track glucose levels, offering a longer-term solution for diabetes management.</p></p>
