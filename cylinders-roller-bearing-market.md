<p><p>The cylindrical roller bearing market is segmented into single row and double row types. Single row cylindrical roller bearings consist of one row of rollers, providing high load capacity and limited space requirements, making them ideal for applications like electric motors. In contrast, double row cylindrical roller bearings feature two rows of rollers, offering enhanced load distribution and stiffness, suitable for applications with heavy loads, such as construction machinery and gearboxes. Both types ensure smooth operation and durability in various industrial applications.</p></p>
