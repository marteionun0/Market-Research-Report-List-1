<p><p>The cylindrical milling cutter market features two primary types: straight teeth and spiral teeth cutters. Straight teeth cutters employ parallel cutting edges, providing a simple design that excels in cutting softer materials with minimal chip size. In contrast, spiral teeth cutters have angled teeth, enhancing cutting efficiency and enabling smoother finishes. This design reduces vibration and improves chip removal, making them suitable for harder materials and complex contours. Both types cater to diverse manufacturing needs in the metalworking industry.</p></p>
