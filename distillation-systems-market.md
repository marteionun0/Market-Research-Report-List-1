<p><p>Distillation systems comprise various market types tailored for specific applications. Fractional distillation separates components based on boiling points, ideal for petrochemical processes. Steam distillation employs steam to extract volatile compounds, commonly used in essential oil production. Vacuum distillation operates under reduced pressure, enabling isolation of heat-sensitive materials. Multiple-effect distillation (MED) utilizes multiple stages to enhance efficiency in water desalination and chemical processing. Other systems may include short-path and molecular distillation, catering to niche market requirements.</p></p>
