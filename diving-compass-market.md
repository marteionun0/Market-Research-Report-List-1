<p><p>The Diving Compass market is segmented into two primary types: Digital Compasses and Pointer Compasses. Digital Compasses utilize electronic sensors to provide precise directional readings and often include additional features like GPS integration and altitude tracking. In contrast, Pointer Compasses are traditional devices that employ magnetic needles to indicate direction. Both types cater to divers' needs for orientation underwater, but Digital Compasses offer enhanced technology while Pointer Compasses appeal to those who prefer a straightforward, mechanical approach.</p></p>
