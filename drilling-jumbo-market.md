<p><p>Drilling jumbos are specialized machines used in underground mining and tunneling. Single-boom jumbos are designed for smaller tunnels, focusing on efficiency and maneuverability. Two-boom models provide increased drilling coverage, suitable for larger projects. Multi-boom jumbos are equipped with several booms, allowing simultaneous drilling operations, ideal for extensive excavation tasks. Each type caters to specific project scales and operational requirements.</p></p>
