<p><p>The external beam radiation therapy market includes several innovative types designed to enhance treatment efficacy and precision. Conformal Radiation Therapy shapes radiation beams to match tumor contours, minimizing damage to surrounding tissues. Proton Beam Therapy utilizes protons to deliver targeted radiation with reduced side effects, ideal for certain cancers. Image Guided Radiation Therapy employs advanced imaging techniques to more accurately deliver radiation, ensuring that the treatment is precisely focused on the tumor, allowing for adaptive treatment strategies as needed.</p></p>
