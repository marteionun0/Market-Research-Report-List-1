<p><ul><li>Single-mode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic jumper market is primarily categorized into two types: single-mode and multimode fibers. Single-mode fibers transmit light directly down a single path, enabling long-distance communication with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunication networks and data centers. In contrast, multimode fibers allow multiple light paths, facilitating shorter distance data transmission, commonly used in local area networks and within buildings. Each type serves distinct applications based on distance, bandwidth, and installation needs, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
