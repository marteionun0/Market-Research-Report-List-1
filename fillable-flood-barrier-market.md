<p><ul><li>Gas Filling</li><li>Liquid/Solid Filling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fillable flood barrier market consists of two primary types based on filling methods: gas filling and liquid/solid filling. Gas filling barriers utilize air or inert gases to create buoyancy and structural integrity, offering quick deployment and lightweight options. In contrast, liquid/solid filling barriers incorporate materials like water or sand that provide weight and stability when filled, making them durable against floodwaters. Each type serves distinct environments and applications, optimizing flood protection according to specific needs and conditions.</p></p>
