<p><strong>The Flatbed Document Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Scanners</li><li>CIS Scanners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flatbed document scanners are categorized mainly into two types: CCD scanners and CIS scanners. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) scanners utilize multiple light sources and sensors to capture high-quality images, making them ideal for scanning artwork and photos. In contrast, CIS (Contact Image Sensor) scanners employ a compact design with a single line of sensors and LED lights, enabling faster scanning of documents with lower power consumption. Both types cater to various needs in offices and homes, emphasizing efficiency and quality.</p></p>
