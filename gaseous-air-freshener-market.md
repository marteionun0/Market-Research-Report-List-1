<p><strong>The Gaseous Air Freshener Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ozone</li><li>Negative Ion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gaseous air freshener market features products primarily categorized into ozone and negative ion generators. Ozone air fresheners work by releasing ozone molecules, which neutralize odors and purify the air, making them effective in eliminating bacteria and odors but requiring careful usage due to potential irritants. Negative ion generators emit negatively charged ions to attract and remove airborne pollutants, allergens, and odor particles, enhancing air quality and promoting a fresher environment without ozone-related concerns. Both types cater to different consumer preferences for air purification.</p></p>
