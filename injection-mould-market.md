<p><p>The injection mold market features several types, primarily including two-plate, three-plate, and stack injection molds. Two-plate molds are the most common, featuring a straightforward design with one parting line, simplifying production. Three-plate molds introduce an additional plate, enabling better control of resin flow and facilitating complex designs. Stack molds, on the other hand, utilize multiple layers of cavities to produce multiple parts simultaneously, enhancing production efficiency and reducing cycle times. Each type serves specific manufacturing needs and product complexities.</p></p>
