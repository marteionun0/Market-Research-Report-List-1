<p><p>The Intelligent Sprinkler Controller market features two primary types: Weather-Based Controllers and Sensor-Based Controllers. Weather-Based Controllers adjust irrigation schedules based on local weather data, optimizing water usage by responding to rainfall and temperature changes. In contrast, Sensor-Based Controllers utilize soil moisture sensors to determine when and how much to irrigate, ensuring precise watering based on the actual needs of the plants. Both types contribute to water conservation and improved landscape health by delivering targeted irrigation solutions.</p></p>
