<p><p>IVIS Imaging Systems encompass various technologies for in vivo imaging. Luminescence Imaging Systems capture light emitted by bioluminescent agents, ideal for studying biological processes. Fluorescence Imaging Systems utilize fluorescent markers to visualize cellular events, providing high-resolution images. Isotope Imaging Systems detect radioactive isotopes, enabling tracking of biological activities at a molecular level. X-Ray Imaging Systems provide detailed anatomical views through X-ray radiation, useful for structural analysis. Other market types may include hybrid systems that combine multiple imaging modalities for comprehensive insights.</p></p>
