<p><p>The laser cutting equipment market can be categorized into two main types: CNC cutting equipment and manual cutting equipment. CNC cutting equipment utilizes computer numerical control technology to automate and enhance precision in cutting processes, making it ideal for large-scale production and complex designs. In contrast, manual cutting equipment relies on operator skill for operation, offering flexibility and lower initial costs, but typically lacks the speed and accuracy of CNC systems. Both types cater to diverse industrial and artistic applications.</p></p>
