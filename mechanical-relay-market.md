<p><p>The mechanical relay market encompasses several types, each serving distinct functions. Signal relays are designed for low-power applications, enabling circuit control with minimal signal interference. Power relays manage high-current loads, essential in industrial and commercial applications. Latching relays maintain their state even when power is removed, offering energy efficiency. Automotive relays specifically cater to vehicle electrical systems, ensuring reliability. Hermetically sealed relays are encased to protect against environmental factors, enhancing durability in demanding applications.</p></p>
