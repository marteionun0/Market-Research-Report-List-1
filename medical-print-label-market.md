<p><strong>The Medical Print Label Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Glued Labels</li><li>Pressure Sensitive/Self Adhesive Labels</li><li>Liner-less labels</li><li>Multi-part Barcode Labels</li><li>In-mold labels</li><li>Shrink Sleeve Label</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical print label market encompasses various types, each catering to specific requirements. Wet glued labels are affixed using an adhesive paste, ideal for healthcare products. Pressure-sensitive/self-adhesive labels feature a sticky backing for easy application. Liner-less labels eliminate the backing for waste reduction. Multi-part barcode labels allow for multiple copies, enhancing tracking. In-mold labels are integrated into packaging during production, providing durability. Shrink sleeve labels conform to container shapes, offering 360-degree branding. Each type serves distinct labeling and regulatory needs in the medical sector.</p></p>
