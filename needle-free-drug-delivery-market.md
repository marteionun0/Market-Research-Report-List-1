<p><strong>The Needle-Free Drug Delivery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Jet-based</li><li>Spring-based</li><li>Laser-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The needle-free drug delivery market comprises three primary types: jet-based, spring-based, and laser-based systems. Jet-based systems utilize a high-pressure stream of liquid to propel medication through the skin. Spring-based devices employ mechanical springs to deliver doses, ensuring consistent and precise administration. Laser-based methods use focused light to create microchannels in the skin, facilitating drug absorption without needles. Each of these technologies enhances patient comfort and compliance, while minimizing the risks associated with traditional needle injections.</p></p>
