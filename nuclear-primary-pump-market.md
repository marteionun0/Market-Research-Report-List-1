<p><strong>The Nuclear Primary Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactor Coolant Pump</li><li>Condensate Pump</li><li>Feed Water Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nuclear Primary Pump market encompasses several key types essential for nuclear power generation. The Reactor Coolant Pump circulates coolant to remove heat from the reactor core, ensuring safe operation. The Condensate Pump recovers and circulates condensate back to the steam generator, optimizing efficiency. The Feed Water Pump supplies water to the steam generator to produce steam for electricity generation. Each pump type plays a critical role in maintaining system integrity, efficiency, and safety in nuclear plants.</p></p>
