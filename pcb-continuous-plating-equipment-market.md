<p><p>The PCB Continuous Plating Equipment market is segmented into two main types: horizontal and vertical plating systems. Horizontal systems involve a layout where the PCBs are processed in a flat orientation, facilitating uniform plating and easier handling of larger panels. In contrast, vertical systems allow PCBs to be suspended, promoting efficient solution flow and reducing the risk of defects. Both types cater to varying production needs, optimizing the plating process for different PCB designs and manufacturing scales.</p></p>
