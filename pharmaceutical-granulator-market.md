<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Granulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li> Dry Granulation</li><li>Moist Granulation </li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical granulator market encompasses two primary types: dry granulation and moist granulation. Dry granulation involves the compression of powder into granules without the use of liquid, making it suitable for heat-sensitive materials. Conversely, moist granulation incorporates a liquid binder to agglomerate powder particles, enhancing flow and compressibility. Both methods are essential in the production of solid dosage forms, each offering distinct advantages that cater to various formulation requirements and manufacturing processes within the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
