<p><ul><li>Plunger Pump</li><li>Piston Pump</li><li>Diaphragm Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reciprocating positive displacement (PD) pumps are categorized into three main types: plunger pumps, piston pumps, and diaphragm pumps. Plunger pumps use a cylindrical plunger for fluid movement, ideal for high-pressure applications. Piston pumps operate similarly but utilize a moving piston within a cylinder, suitable for varied viscosity fluids. Diaphragm pumps employ flexible diaphragms to create suction and discharge, making them effective for corrosive or abrasive liquids. Each type serves specific industrial needs, emphasizing efficiency and reliability in fluid handling.</p></p>
