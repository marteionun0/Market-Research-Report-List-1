<p><p>The refrigerant pump market is categorized into two main types: open type and closed type refrigerant pumps. Open type refrigerant pumps feature components exposed to the environment, typically allowing for easier maintenance and larger capacity handling but may lead to potential losses in efficiency. Conversely, closed type refrigerant pumps are sealed, offering improved efficiency, reduced refrigerant loss, and protection against contamination, making them ideal for applications requiring higher reliability and lower environmental impact. Both types serve critical roles in HVAC systems.</p></p>
