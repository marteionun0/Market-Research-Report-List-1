<p><p>The rheometers market comprises several types, each serving distinct purposes in measuring material flow properties. Dynamic rheometers assess the behavior of materials under oscillatory stress, ideal for complex fluids. Capillary rheometers measure the viscosity of polymers and other materials through a capillary tube, useful in quality control. Torque rheometers evaluate the viscosity and processing behavior of materials, focusing on the torque required during mixing and compounding. Other rheometer types include instruments tailored for specialized applications or conditions.</p></p>
