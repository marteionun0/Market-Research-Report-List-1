<p><p>The semi-solid battery market can be categorized into two main types based on the electrolyte used: organic electrolytes and inorganic electrolytes. Organic electrolyte batteries utilize carbon-based compounds, offering advantages like high energy density and enhanced safety due to reduced flammability. In contrast, inorganic electrolyte batteries employ metal salts and other inorganic materials, providing benefits such as better thermal stability and broader operating temperature ranges. Both types aim to improve battery performance, longevity, and sustainability for various applications.</p></p>
