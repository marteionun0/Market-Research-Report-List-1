<p><ul><li>High Temperature Vulcanised (HTV)</li><li>Room Temperature Vulcanised (RTV)</li><li>Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicone elastomers market encompasses three main types: High Temperature Vulcanised (HTV), Room Temperature Vulcanised (RTV), and Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR). HTV is known for its durability and resistance to extreme temperatures, making it ideal for automotive and industrial applications. RTV cures at room temperature, offering convenience for diverse uses, including sealants and adhesives. LSR, a two-part silicone system, effectively molds intricate shapes, widely used in healthcare and consumer products due to its flexibility and biocompatibility.</p></p>
