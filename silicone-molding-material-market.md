<p><p>The silicone molding material market encompasses various techniques, primarily press molding and injection molding. Press molding involves placing silicone material in a heated mold, where pressure is applied to shape the final product, ideal for larger components. In contrast, injection molding involves injecting liquid silicone into a mold under high pressure, allowing for precise and intricate designs, suitable for high-volume production. Both methods leverage the versatility of silicone to meet diverse applications, from automotive parts to medical devices.</p></p>
