<p><p>The siphonic drainage system market is categorized into two main types: internal and external drainage systems. Internal drainage systems are designed to manage water accumulation within a structure, utilizing siphonic principles to transport water through pipes with minimal slope, optimizing space and efficiency. In contrast, external drainage systems focus on directing surface water away from a building or site to prevent flooding and erosion. Both systems leverage siphonic technology for efficient fluid management in various applications.</p></p>
