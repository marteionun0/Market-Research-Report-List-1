<p><p>The Smart Gate market includes three primary identification types: Password Identification, Card Identification, and Biometric Identification. Password Identification relies on user-entered passwords for access control, offering straightforward security but vulnerable to hacking. Card Identification uses physical cards, such as RFID or magnetic stripe cards, for secure entry, balancing convenience and security. Biometric Identification leverages unique physical traits, like fingerprints or facial recognition, providing enhanced security through difficult-to-replicate biometrics, making it a preferred choice for high-security applications.</p></p>
