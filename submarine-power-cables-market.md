<p><strong>The Submarine Power Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Core Cable</li><li>Multi Core Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The submarine power cables market is primarily categorized into single-core and multi-core cables. Single-core cables consist of one conductor and are typically used for high voltage direct current (HVDC) applications, offering simplicity and ease of installation. In contrast, multi-core cables contain multiple conductors, allowing for greater transmission capacity and flexibility in alternating current (AC) applications. Both types are essential for undersea energy transmission, connecting offshore wind farms and other renewable energy sources to onshore grids efficiently.</p></p>
