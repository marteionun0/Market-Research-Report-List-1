<p><p>The thin film ceramic substrates market includes various types, each with unique properties. Alumina (Al2O3) is widely used for its excellent electrical insulation and thermal conductivity. Aluminium Nitride (AlN) offers superior thermal performance and is suitable for high-power applications. Beryllium Oxide (BeO) provides exceptional thermal conductivity but is limited by safety concerns. Silicon Nitride (Si3N4) is recognized for its mechanical strength and thermal shock resistance, making it ideal for demanding environments. Each substrate type caters to specific applications in electronics and engineering.</p></p>
